About

I am Chelsie, a data analyst with special interest in business intelligence. I want to help organizations move toward making data-driven decisions. I enjoy having conversations about the types of problems organizations are currently facing and how data can be captured, manipulated, analyzed, and presented to make their employees more effective and more efficient.

Throughout school, mathematics courses were my favorite. Math's focus on logic, formulas, and proofs lend themselves well to developing critical thinking skills. They gave me the skills needed to take large problems and break them down into manageable pieces so that a solution could be found. Data analysis allows me to apply my love of problems with my mathematics background across numerous contexts. From large-scale projects such as population migration to smaller-scale items like analyzing an organization's annual sales, data analysis can give business leaders insights they might otherwise overlook.
